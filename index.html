What We Do

Complete Automotive Glass Solutions

We supply and fit every category of automotive glass — for passenger cars, SUVs, buses, trucks, vans, earth movers and heavy commercial vehicles. OEM-grade precision, every single job.

Windshield

OEM-grade laminated glass, precision-bonded with urethane for a leak-proof, rattle-free finish.

Door Side Glass

Tempered safety glass for all door positions — power-window regulator check included.

Fixed Glass

Non-opening panels — quarter glasses, vent windows and fixed rear side panes.

Rear Glass

Replacement with full defogger grid reconnection, antenna integration and electrical testing.

Rubber Fitting

Precision rubber beading and gasket seals fitted around glass panels for a weather-tight, rattle-free result.

Sliding Glass

Smooth-sliding panels for buses, vans and commercial vehicles — track, runner and seal replacement included.

Fibre Glass

Specialist fibre-reinforced panels for industrial vehicles, earth movers and heavy-duty custom applications.

Emergency Door Glass

Purpose-built emergency exit glazing for buses and coaches — safety-rated, compliant and quick-release.

Safety Glass

Laminated and toughened safety glass — engineered to absorb impact without shattering, for every application.

Quality of Work

How We Install Your Glass

Every installation follows our 8-step quality standard — refined over 30 years of hands-on experience. No shortcuts, no guesswork. Just the precision that keeps you safe on the road.

Glass Measurement

Precision measurement with calibrated tools for zero-gap fit.

Frame Preparation

Old adhesive and debris cleaned for a flawless bond surface.

Glass Cutting

Exact dimensions, smooth chip-free edges every time.

Rubber Beading

Watertight gaskets sealed around the glass perimeter.

Adhesive Application

OEM-grade polyurethane applied in precise bead pattern.

Glass Fitting

Positioned with suction tools, pressed firm for full contact.

Alignment & Finishing

Flush fit verified, edges polished to factory standard.

Leak Testing

Water-pressure tested — leaves only after a 100% pass.
